What’s inside

include/dli/settings.hpp — settings singleton (DELETED/EXACT/TALK/SAFETY/EXCLUSIVE, etc.)

src/cli/settings.cpp — implementation of dli::settings()

include/dli/console.hpp — dli::clear_screen() decl

src/cli/console_clear.cpp — cross-platform clear-screen

include/dli/normalize.hpp — ALLTRIM() + optional UPPER() (EXACT respected)

src/cli/cmd_wsreport.cpp — complete command (works with dli::api_*; fallbacks included)

include/dli/shell_api_extras.hpp — decls for index-field names/values

src/cli/shell_api_extras.cpp — stub impls (safe until you wire real access)

include/dli/status_addon.hpp — plug-in helper to extend STATUS (ALL/SETTINGS/no-arg default)

There’s a short README.txt in the zip that shows exactly where to include the status_addon.hpp and how to hook the clear-screen calls on startup and after BROWSE/BROWSETUI.

Quick integrate (TL;DR)

Copy include/dli/* → your include/dli/ and src/cli/* → your src/cli/.

In cmd_status.cpp:

#include "dli/status_addon.hpp"
// ...
// at the very top of cmd_STATUS(...)
extern void cmd_STATUS(xbase::DbArea&, std::istringstream&);   // existing
extern void cmd_WSREPORT(xbase::DbArea&, std::istringstream&); // new
if (dli_status_predispatch(area, iss, &cmd_STATUS, &cmd_WSREPORT)) return;


(Optional) In main.cpp before first prompt, call dli::clear_screen();

(Optional) At the end of cmd_browse.cpp and cmd_browsetui.cpp, call dli::clear_screen();

Reconfigure & build.
