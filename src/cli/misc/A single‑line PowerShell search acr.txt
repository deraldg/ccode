A single‑line PowerShell search across your whole repo looks like this:

```powershell
Get-ChildItem -Recurse -File | Select-String -Pattern "Failed to open index:"
```

This scans every file under the current directory, recurses into subfolders, and prints matching filename, line number, and text.

---

### Variants you may find useful

- **Limit to source files only (.cpp/.hpp/.ps1/etc.):**
```powershell
Get-ChildItem -Recurse -Include *.cpp,*.hpp,*.h,*.ps1 -File |
    Select-String "Failed to open index:"
```

- **Show only filenames that contain the match:**
```powershell
Get-ChildItem -Recurse -File |
    Select-String "Failed to open index:" |
    Select-Object -ExpandProperty Path -Unique
```

- **Case‑insensitive (default) but explicit:**
```powershell
Select-String -Pattern "Failed to open index:" -CaseInsensitive -Path (Get-ChildItem -Recurse -File).FullName
```
